Who We Are

A Legacy of Comfort & Excellence

Galilee Gardens Hotels Ltd — trading as Galilee Hotel — is a premier hospitality establishment proudly rooted in the vibrant city of Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. We were founded with a singular vision: to create a sanctuary of comfort, luxury, and genuine Nigerian warmth in the heart of the Niger Delta's economic capital.

Located at 65 Old Refinery Road, Elelenwo, our hotel is strategically positioned to serve business travellers, tourists, and event guests who seek more than just a place to sleep — they seek an experience. Every corner of Galilee Hotel has been thoughtfully designed to reflect elegance, culture, and world-class standards.

With professionally trained staff, state-of-the-art facilities, and a relentless commitment to excellence, Galilee Hotel has become the preferred choice for executives, families, and dignitaries visiting Port Harcourt. We believe that every guest deserves to feel valued, and we deliver on that promise — every single day.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide every guest with an unparalleled hospitality experience — combining luxurious accommodation, exceptional cuisine, and heartfelt service that honours our African heritage while meeting the highest global standards.


We are committed to being the best hotel in Rivers State by continuously innovating, investing in our people, and ensuring that every moment of your stay is memorable, comfortable, and truly extraordinary.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and celebrated hospitality brand in Nigeria — a hotel where guests return not just for the luxury, but for the feeling of being truly at home, cared for, and celebrated.


We envision a future where Galilee Hotel is synonymous with excellence across West Africa, setting the benchmark for hospitality quality, sustainability, and guest satisfaction in every community we serve.
