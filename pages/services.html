In Detail

Our Services, Up Close

Luxury Accommodation

Every room at Galilee Hotel is a sanctuary. From our Standard Rooms to the magnificent Presidential Suite, all spaces feature premium mattresses, high-thread-count linens, climate control, flat-screen TVs, and carefully curated décor that blends contemporary elegance with Nigerian warmth. Our housekeeping team ensures impeccable standards every single day.

Restaurant & Fine Dining

Our in-house restaurant is a culinary destination in its own right. Led by our talented Executive Chef, the menu celebrates the rich flavours of Nigerian cuisine alongside refined international dishes. From freshly prepared jollof rice and afang soup to continental breakfast spreads and à la carte dining — every meal at Galilee Hotel is a memorable occasion. Private dining arrangements available upon request.

Event Hall & Conferences

Our versatile event hall is the premier venue for corporate events, conferences, product launches, weddings, and social gatherings in Port Harcourt. Equipped with state-of-the-art audio-visual technology, flexible seating arrangements, professional sound systems, and our dedicated events team to manage every detail — from décor to catering. Capacity: up to 300 guests. Contact us for event packages.

Airport Pickup & Transfer

Start your Galilee Hotel experience the moment you land. Our premium airport pickup service provides a comfortable, punctual transfer from Port Harcourt International Airport (PHC) directly to the hotel — and back again at departure. Executive and Presidential Suite guests enjoy this service complimentarily. Other guests may arrange transfers at a competitive flat rate. Advance booking required.

24/7 Room Service

Whether you desire a midnight snack, a full breakfast in bed, or urgent amenities delivered to your door, our room service team is on hand around the clock. Simply dial our front desk and your request will be handled promptly and professionally. Our extended room service menu features hot meals, beverages, snacks, and essential toiletries available at any hour.
